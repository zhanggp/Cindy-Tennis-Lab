# Serve Scoring Rubric

Use scores to guide attention, not to judge the learner.

| Area | Score | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Toss | 0-100 | Height, placement, rhythm |
| Side-on position | 0-100 | Body alignment and shoulder turn |
| Trophy position | 0-100 | Arm shape, balance, timing |
| Leg drive | 0-100 | Smooth upward and forward energy |
| Contact point | 0-100 | Height, reach, consistency |
| Follow-through | 0-100 | Relaxed finish and safe deceleration |

## Review Rule

Only choose one main correction per review.
