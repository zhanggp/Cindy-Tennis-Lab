# Serve Drills

## Toss Repetition

Goal: stable height and landing point.

Volume: 100 tosses without hitting.

## Trophy Hold

Goal: feel side-on balance and racket position.

Volume: 50 repetitions, hold for 2 seconds.

## Smooth Full Motion

Goal: relaxed sequence.

Cue: jump up and send the ball out.

Measure: consistency before speed.
