# Mickey Roadmap

## Stage 1: Stabilize

Reduce avoidable errors and build repeatable rally patterns.

## Stage 2: Understand

Connect technique, spacing, timing, and decision making.

## Stage 3: Pressure

Build patterns that work in matches, not only practice.

## Stage 4: Compete At 4.0

Develop serve, return, rally tolerance, net play, fitness, and match planning
into a coherent adult game.
