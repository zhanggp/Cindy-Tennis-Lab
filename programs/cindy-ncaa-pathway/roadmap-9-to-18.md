# Cindy Roadmap: 9 To 18

## 9-10: Build Action Models

Stabilize movement quality. Do not chase speed.

## 10-11: Build Kinetic Chain

Add shoulder turn, weight transfer, and landing patterns.

## 11-12: Build Spin Feel

Introduce kick serve and slice serve as feel development.

## 12-14: Build Athletic Base

Increase strength, core control, and explosive capacity with age-appropriate
training.

## 14-16: Build Match Systems

Develop serve patterns, return patterns, point construction, and pressure
habits.

## 16-18: Build College Readiness

Turn skills into weapons, habits into independence, and records into a clear
development story.
