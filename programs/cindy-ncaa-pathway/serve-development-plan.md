# Serve Development Plan

## Principle

At age 9, the serve goal is not speed. The goal is a stable model that can grow
with the body.

## Four-Line Cue

抛得稳。

站得侧。

蹬得顺。

挥得松。

## Monthly Review

Capture front, side, and rear videos once per month.

Review one priority at a time:

- toss consistency
- trophy position
- body side-on position
- smooth leg drive
- relaxed swing
- contact height
- follow-through and landing
