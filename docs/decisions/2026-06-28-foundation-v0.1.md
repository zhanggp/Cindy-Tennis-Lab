# Decision: Foundation v0.1

Date: 2026-06-28

## Decision

Cindy Tennis Lab starts as a long-term learning system rather than a single
course, app, or AI agent.

## Context

The founding conversation centered on tennis, AI, parent companionship, and the
belief that understanding is more important than imitation.

The first concrete learners are:

- Cindy: junior tennis learner, NCAA pathway.
- Mickey: adult tennis learner, 2.5 to 4.0.

## Consequences

The repository should protect `WHY.md`, keep philosophy visible, and organize
future work around reusable knowledge, learner programs, applied labs, evidence
records, and journals.

The project should grow through issues and commits, not scattered chat alone.
