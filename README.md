# Cindy Tennis Lab

Cindy Tennis Lab is a long-term learning lab for tennis, family companionship,
and AI-assisted growth.

It starts from a simple belief:

> Understanding is more important than imitation.
>
> 理解，比模仿更重要。

We use AI to help learners see patterns, ask better questions, connect evidence
to practice, and turn daily training into a living knowledge system.

AI does not replace learning. AI amplifies learning.

AI does not replace parents. AI amplifies companionship.

Build with AI. Grow together.

AI时代，一起探索，积极前行。

## Learners

### Mickey

Mickey is an adult tennis learner. The current long-term goal is to grow from a
2.5 level player toward 4.0 through better understanding, disciplined practice,
video review, evidence-driven training, and continuous iteration.

### Cindy

Cindy is a junior tennis learner. The long-term direction is an NCAA pathway:
building technique, athletic foundation, match intelligence, learning habits,
academic balance, and resilient love for the game.
