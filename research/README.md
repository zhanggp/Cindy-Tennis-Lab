# Research

Research stores public notes and source-oriented work.

It does not store raw copyrighted books, paid ebooks, private PDFs, or raw video
libraries.

Use research folders to turn sources into notes, then extract claims into
`knowledge/claims/`.

## Areas

- `papers/`: scientific papers and preprints.
- `official/`: official coaching, safety, and pathway systems.
- `coaching-frameworks/`: high-quality practice frameworks.
- `video-analysis/`: video understanding, annotation, and future parser work.
