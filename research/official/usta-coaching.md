## Extracted Observations

- USTA maintains a Coach & Organize area that points coaches and organizers to
  resources, tools, support, and education.
- USTA Coaching presents coaching packages and learning paths for different
  coach types.
- USTA Safe Play centers athlete safety around education, screening, reporting
  tools, policies, and appropriate conduct.
- Safe Play approval includes policy acknowledgement, SafeSport training, and a
  background check process.

## Claim Candidates

- Coaching systems should include safety, education, and professional
  development, not only tactical or technical instruction.
- Cindy Tennis Lab should keep parent, coach, and professional safety roles
  explicit.
- Any real-world coaching workflow involving minors should include safeguarding
  awareness and escalation paths.
