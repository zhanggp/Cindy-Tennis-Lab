## Extracted Observations

- World Tennis / ITF frames coach education as important across recreational
  and professional tennis.
- The coach education programme supports national associations through courses,
  conferences, online learning, publications, and multilingual resources.
- The World Tennis Academy is positioned as an online tennis education hub with
  courses and a content library.
- The Coaching and Sports Science Review is a tennis-specific publication source
  for topics such as sport psychology, physiology, drills, tactics, technique,
  and biomechanics.
