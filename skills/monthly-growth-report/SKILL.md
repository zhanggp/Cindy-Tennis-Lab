# Monthly Growth Report

Create a monthly report from evidence.

## Inputs

- learner
- month
- practice notes
- match notes
- video review notes
- injury or workload notes
- previous month's focus

## Workflow

1. Summarize the month in plain language.
2. Separate evidence from interpretation.
3. Identify progress, risks, and open questions.
4. Choose one primary focus for next month.
5. Connect the focus to the learner's long-term path.

## Output Format

```markdown
## Month Summary

## Evidence Reviewed

## Progress

## Risks Or Watch Items

## Open Questions

## Next Month Focus

## Parent Or Coach Notes
```

## Guardrails

- Do not pretend missing evidence exists.
- Do not rank the learner's value by results.
- Keep the tone calm, specific, and long-term.
