# Serve Video Review

Review serve video as a learning companion, not as a judge.

## Inputs

- learner
- date
- age or level
- video angle: front, side, rear, or mixed
- stated focus
- observable evidence

## Workflow

1. Identify the visible serve phase: setup, toss, trophy, loading, leg drive,
   contact, follow-through, landing.
2. Name one strength supported by visible evidence.
3. Name one issue supported by visible evidence.
4. Choose one next practice focus.
5. Avoid changing multiple movement patterns at once.

## Guardrails

- Do not diagnose injury.
- Do not over-focus on speed for young learners.
- For Cindy, prefer action stability and health over power.
- If evidence is unclear, say what angle or clip is needed next.
