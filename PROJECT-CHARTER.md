# Cindy Tennis Lab Project Charter v1.0

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Date: 2026-06-30

## 1. Why

AI 时代，一个工程师父亲如何利用 AI，与女儿共同成长，并探索复杂技能学习的新范式。

网球，是第一个实验场。

不是最终目标。

## 2. Mission

> 利用 AI 和第一性原理，让复杂技能学习变得可理解、可验证、可陪伴。

Three keywords:

- Understand
- Verify
- Grow Together

## 3. Vision

Build a Learning Operating System, or LOS.

First stage:

- Tennis

Future:

```text
Learning OS

├── Tennis
├── English
├── Programming
├── AI
├── Reading
└── Fitness
```

## 5. Core Principles

### First Principles

Always ask why.

### Evidence Driven

Every claim needs evidence.

### Engineering Thinking

Knowledge can be modeled.

### Long-termism

Design for what should still make sense ten years from now.

### Human First

AI amplifies learning.

AI amplifies companionship.

AI does not replace either.
