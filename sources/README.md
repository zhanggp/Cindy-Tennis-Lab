# Sources

Sources are the registered inputs to Cindy Tennis Lab.

The goal is not to collect files. The goal is to collect traceable claims.

```text
Source
↓
Claim
↓
Knowledge Node
↓
Experiment
↓
Validation
↓
Training
```

## Policy

- Public repositories store metadata, notes, claims, schemas, and allowed links.
- Public repositories do not store full copyrighted books, paid ebooks, private
  PDFs, raw video libraries, or medical/private records.
- Private materials can be registered as private sources.
- Private sources should contribute structured claims and personal notes, not
  copied raw text.

## Tiers

| Tier | Meaning | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| T0 | Own data | Highest-value evidence from Mickey, Cindy, training, matches, video, coach feedback |
| T1 | Scientific research | Explains why something may work |
| T2 | Official systems | Establishes mainstream coaching and safety standards |
| T3 | Practice frameworks | Provides coaching experience and skill taxonomies |
| T4 | Professional cases | Shows high-level implementation variants |
| T5 | Content inspiration | Supports storytelling, animation, and teaching expression |

## Files

- `registry.yaml`: public source registry.
- `agent-source-map.md`: maps virtual team roles to required source classes.
- `private-sources.example.yaml`: example format for private sources.
