# Biomechanics

Biomechanics helps explain how the body creates efficient tennis movement.

## Cindy

For Cindy, biomechanics should protect long-term development. The goal is to
build movement quality without overloading a growing body.

## Mickey

For Mickey, biomechanics should make adult learning clearer. The goal is to
understand what change is realistic, useful, and safe.

## Evidence

Video, body feedback, coach feedback, injury history, and repeated movement
patterns should be connected before making strong claims.
