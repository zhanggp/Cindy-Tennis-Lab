# Serve Ontology

The serve ontology defines how Cindy Tennis Lab understands the tennis serve.

## Core Sequence

1. Setup
2. Toss
3. Trophy position
4. Loading
5. Leg drive
6. Racket drop
7. Contact
8. Follow-through
9. Landing and recovery

## Development Principle

For Cindy, the early goal is action model stability, not speed.

抛得稳。站得侧。蹬得顺。挥得松。
