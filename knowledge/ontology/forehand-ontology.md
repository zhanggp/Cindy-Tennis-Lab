# Forehand Ontology

The forehand ontology describes how to observe, explain, and train forehand
development.

## Core Questions

- Is preparation early enough?
- Is spacing stable?
- Is contact point repeatable?
- Is balance protected through contact?
- Does recovery support the next ball?

## Evidence

Use video, rally consistency, depth, direction control, and match outcomes.

## Next Expansion

Define unit turn, spacing, swing path, contact, finish, recovery, and tactical
forehand patterns.
