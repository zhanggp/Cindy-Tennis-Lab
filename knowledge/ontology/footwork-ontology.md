# Footwork Ontology

The footwork ontology describes movement as a decision and timing system, not
only fast feet.

## Core Questions

- Does the player read the ball early?
- Is the first step efficient?
- Is spacing created before the swing?
- Is balance protected through contact?
- Does recovery prepare the next shot?

## Evidence

Use rally video, court positioning, recovery patterns, late-contact frequency,
and balance errors.

## Next Expansion

Define split step, adjustment steps, open stance, neutral stance, recovery, and
transition movement.
