# Backhand Ontology

The backhand ontology describes how to observe, explain, and train backhand
development.

## Core Questions

- Is the shoulder turn early?
- Is the player balanced before contact?
- Is the contact point in the correct strike zone?
- Can the player control height and depth?
- Can the backhand defend and attack?

## Evidence

Use side-view video, rally tolerance, target accuracy, depth control, and match
patterns under pressure.

## Next Expansion

Define two-handed backhand mechanics, slice backhand, defensive backhand, and
backhand transition patterns.
