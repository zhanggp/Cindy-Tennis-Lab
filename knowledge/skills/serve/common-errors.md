# Serve Common Errors

Common errors are observable breakdowns, not character judgments.

## v0.1 Error Map

| Error | Likely Layer | Observable Signal | Next Question |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Inconsistent toss | Motor Learning / Biomechanics | Ball moves across different contact windows | Is the toss target defined? |
| Facing too front early | Biomechanics | Body loses side-on shape before contact | Is the setup repeatable? |
| Arm-only serve | Biomechanics | Legs and trunk do not contribute smoothly | Is loading too complex or rushed? |
| Low contact | Physics / Biomechanics | Ball has low margin or forced push | Is the toss too low or too close? |
| Falling forward or sideways | Anatomy / Biomechanics | Player cannot hold landing balance | Is the player chasing the toss or overloaded? |
| Slow recovery | Skill / Motor Learning | Player watches serve and is late for next ball | Is recovery part of the drill rule? |
| Speed chasing | Metrics | Player serves harder but loses consistency | Is speed being measured too early? |

## Use

Use this map to choose one next question.

Do not turn every error into a correction at the same time.
