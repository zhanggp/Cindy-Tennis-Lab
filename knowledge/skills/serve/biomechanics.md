# Serve Biomechanics

Biomechanics describes how the body coordinates the serve action.

## Core Sequence

| Phase | Observable Question |
| --- | --- |
| Setup | Is the starting shape repeatable? |
| Toss | Does the toss create a stable hitting window? |
| Trophy | Does the body reach a balanced side-on shape? |
| Loading | Do legs, hips, trunk, and shoulders prepare together? |
| Upward drive | Does energy move upward and forward smoothly? |
| Racket drop | Does the racket accelerate without forced tension? |
| Contact | Is contact high, extended, and balanced? |
| Follow-through | Does the body decelerate safely? |
| Landing | Can the player land balanced? |
| Recovery | Is the player ready for the next ball? |

## v0.1 Constraints

For Cindy, the serve model should protect:

- balance
- rhythm
- shoulder comfort
- foot and heel tolerance
- recovery into the next ball
