# Parenting With AI

AI does not replace parents. AI amplifies companionship.

## Parent Role

The parent role is presence, patience, protection, encouragement, and memory.

A parent does not need to become a full-time coach. A parent can become a better
observer, question asker, record keeper, and companion.

## AI Role

AI can help summarize evidence, compare patterns, generate review questions,
organize practice plans, and make learning easier to revisit.

AI should not become the emotional center of the journey.

## Companionship Principle

Build with AI. Grow together.

AI时代，一起探索，积极前行。

The most important output is not a perfect training plan. The most important
output is a healthier, clearer, more durable way to grow together.
