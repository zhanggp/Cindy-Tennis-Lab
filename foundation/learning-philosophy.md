# Learning Philosophy

Understanding is more important than imitation.

理解，比模仿更重要。

## Learn From Principles

Start with why the movement, tactic, or habit works.

For tennis, this means asking about balance, spacing, timing, contact point,
body sequence, perception, decision, and recovery before chasing surface form.

## Learn From Evidence

Evidence can come from video, coach feedback, practice notes, match notes,
physical feeling, or repeated patterns.

One observation is a clue. A repeated pattern is a signal.

## Learn Through Iteration

The lab should improve through small experiments.

Do not try to fix everything at once. Choose one focus, test it, review it, and
then choose the next focus.

## Learn For The Long Term

Short-term results matter, but they should not damage long-term growth.

For Cindy, the goal is not to serve fast at age 9. The goal is to enter later
stages with a healthy, understandable, adaptable technical foundation.
