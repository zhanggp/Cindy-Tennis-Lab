# Vision

Cindy Tennis Lab exists to explore how tennis learning can become more
understandable, more measurable, and more human in the AI era.

The vision is not to build a shortcut around effort. The vision is to build a
better learning environment around effort.

## Core Belief

Understanding is more important than imitation.

理解，比模仿更重要。

A learner can copy a swing shape without knowing why it works. A parent can
record every match without knowing what changed. A coach can give advice that
sounds right but is hard to test.

Cindy Tennis Lab aims to connect these pieces:

- concepts to evidence
- practice to feedback
- video to learning notes
- match outcomes to training decisions
- parent companionship to long-term growth

## AI Role

AI does not replace learning. AI amplifies learning.

AI does not replace parents. AI amplifies companionship.

AI can help organize observations, detect patterns, summarize progress, compare
training plans, and generate better questions. But the learner still learns, the
player still plays, and the parent still shows up.

## Long-Term Direction

Build with AI. Grow together.

AI时代，一起探索，积极前行。

This lab will grow slowly and honestly: one principle, one schema, one video,
one practice note, one experiment, and one reflection at a time.
