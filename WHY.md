# Why

Tennis is a beautiful learning problem.

It is physical, technical, tactical, emotional, and strategic. Every rally
contains feedback, but feedback is often noisy. Every practice creates data, but
data is often forgotten. Every match reveals patterns, but patterns are hard to
see when everyone is tired or emotional.

Cindy Tennis Lab exists because learning deserves a better memory.

## Why Understanding Matters

Imitation can help a beginner get started. But imitation alone is fragile.

Understanding gives a learner the ability to adapt:

- when the ball is faster
- when the opponent changes tactics
- when the body feels different
- when pressure rises
- when a coach is not nearby

Understanding is more important than imitation.

理解，比模仿更重要。

## Why AI Matters

AI can help turn scattered experience into structured learning.

It can help review notes, organize video observations, compare hypotheses,
surface recurring patterns, and suggest better questions for the next practice.

AI does not replace learning. AI amplifies learning.

AI does not replace parents. AI amplifies companionship.

## Why This Repository Exists

This repository is the shared foundation for building a tennis learning system.
It keeps principles, architecture, schemas, experiments, and journals in one
place so the lab can grow with memory and discipline.
