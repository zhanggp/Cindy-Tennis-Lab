# Episode 001 Brief

Status: Draft

Working title:

理解，比模仿更重要

English idea:

Understanding is more important than imitation.

## Purpose

Introduce Cindy Tennis Lab as a learning experiment, not a tennis-tip account.

The first episode should explain the method:

```text
Source
->
Claim
->
Knowledge
->
Experiment
->
Validation
```

## Core Message

AI should not replace parents, coaches, or practice.

AI can help a family ask better questions, organize evidence, and grow together.

## Angle

Most people ask:

> Can AI teach tennis?

Cindy Tennis Lab asks:

> Can AI help an ordinary family learn a complex skill more scientifically?

## Public-Safe Story Frame

Use a generic junior match-preparation context:

- before a junior match, do not overload the child
- choose one cue
- observe a few signals
- review gently afterward

Do not mention real event dates, locations, opponents, or private details.

## Do Not Include

- a training plan
- technical instruction
- private match data
- real schedule details
- opponent names
- coach private comments
- medical or pain details
- claims that imply AI can replace a coach or parent

## Possible Structure

1. Hook: Tennis is not just copying strokes.
2. Problem: Parents often see results, not learning signals.
3. Method: Source, Claim, Knowledge, Experiment, Validation.
4. Example: For a junior match, observe serve rhythm, return readiness, reset,
   and enjoyment.
5. Close: Understanding is more important than imitation.
