# Manifesto

We believe tennis learning should be understood, not merely copied.

We believe parents can become better companions when they have better tools,
better language, and better patience.

We believe AI should make learning deeper, not shallower.

We believe progress is built through first principles, evidence, engineering
thinking, continuous iteration, and long-termism.

## What We Stand For

Understanding is more important than imitation.

理解，比模仿更重要。

AI does not replace learning. AI amplifies learning.

AI does not replace parents. AI amplifies companionship.

Build with AI. Grow together.

AI时代，一起探索，积极前行。

## What We Practice

- Start from first principles before chasing quick fixes.
- Observe evidence before making strong claims.
- Treat training as a system, not a collection of random drills.
- Iterate continuously instead of waiting for perfect plans.
- Protect long-term growth over short-term performance anxiety.

## Who This Is For

This lab begins with two learners:

- Mickey: an adult tennis learner moving from 2.5 toward 4.0.
- Cindy: a junior tennis learner building toward a possible NCAA pathway.

Their journeys are different, but the method is shared: understand the game,
study the evidence, train deliberately, and grow together.
